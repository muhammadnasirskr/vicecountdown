Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which ones Vice Countdown uses, and how you can control them. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make sites work, to remember preferences, and to measure and serve advertising. Similar technologies such as pixels, local storage and device identifiers work in comparable ways; we refer to all of these simply as "cookies" here.

Types of cookies we use

Strictly necessary

These are required for the site to function and to be served securely. They do not track you for advertising and cannot be switched off through the site.

Analytics / performance

These help us understand aggregate, anonymised traffic — for example which pages are most read — so we can improve the site. They do not identify you personally to us.

Advertising

We display ads through Google AdSense. Google and its partners may set cookies (including the DoubleClick DART cookie) to serve and measure ads, and to show more relevant advertising based on your visits to this and other sites. Details and opt-out links are in our Privacy Policy.

Third-party cookies

Some cookies are set by third parties we rely on. The main ones are:

  • Google AdSense / DoubleClick — advertising delivery and measurement. See Google's advertising policy.
  • Google Fonts — our typography is loaded from Google Fonts, which may receive your IP address as part of the request.

How to control cookies

You can manage or delete cookies at any time:

  • Adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies. Every major browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge) has a privacy or cookie section in its settings.
  • Opt out of personalised advertising at Google Ads Settings, aboutads.info/choices, or youronlinechoices.eu.
  • Use your browser's "Do Not Track" or private browsing mode.

Please note that blocking some cookies may affect how the site works or which ads you see, but the countdown and core content will still function.

Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy as our tools change. Updates are reflected in the "last updated" date above.
