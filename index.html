What we know

The confirmed picture

A plain-language summary of what has been officially shared — no leaks, no rumours.

Setting

Back to Vice City

The game returns to a sun-soaked, neon-drenched world inspired by Vice City and the fictional state of Leonida — Rockstar's take on modern-day Florida.

Protagonists

Lucia & Jason

Two central characters, Lucia and Jason, anchor the story — the first time a mainline Grand Theft Auto has centred a playable female lead alongside her counterpart.

Experience

Single-player at launch

Rockstar has described the launch as a single-player experience. There is no announced date for a separate online mode at release.

Release history

Delayed twice

The launch window moved from an original Autumn 2025 target to May 26, 2026, and then to the current date of November 19, 2026, for extra polish.

Demand

Record-level interest

Early signals were huge: the physical version reportedly sold out on Amazon US in roughly an hour, with PS5 pre-orders said to be outselling Xbox by around six to one.

Expectations

A historic launch

Some industry analysts have suggested GTA 6 could move on the order of 20 million copies in a single day, which would rank among the biggest entertainment launches ever.

Spot the fakes

How to verify GTA 6 news

GTA 6 attracts more fake "leaks" than almost any game in history. Here's a quick way to check whether a claim is real.

  • Trust Rockstar Newswire first. Official announcements are published there before anywhere else.
  • Check Take-Two investor updates for release timing and business details — these are legally sensitive and reliable.
  • Be careful with retailer screenshots. Placeholder dates and prices on store pages are frequently wrong.
  • Don't treat social media rumours as confirmation. A viral post is not a source.
  • Check whether a source links back to the original Rockstar statement. If it can't, be sceptical.
✓

A real update should trace back to Rockstar, Take-Two, PlayStation or Xbox — especially for release timing, PC details, editions, pricing and performance claims.

FAQ

Common questions

When is GTA 6 releasing?

Rockstar Games has set the Grand Theft Auto VI release date for Thursday, November 19, 2026, on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S.

Is the November 19 date confirmed?

Yes. November 19, 2026 is the date confirmed in an official Rockstar Newswire announcement. Any other date circulating on social media should be treated as unverified.

Is GTA 6 coming to PC?

A PC version has not been announced. The confirmed launch platforms are PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S. Rockstar has historically brought Grand Theft Auto titles to PC after the console release, but nothing about GTA 6 on PC is official.

Is GTA 6 coming to Nintendo Switch 2?

No Nintendo Switch 2 version has been announced. The only confirmed platforms are PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S.

How much does GTA 6 cost?

In the United States, the Standard Edition is $79.99 and the Ultimate Edition is $99.99, based on Rockstar's pricing.

Does the Ultimate Edition give early access?

No. The Ultimate Edition adds digital content such as extra vehicles, weapons and cosmetic items, but it does not let you play the game before the November 19, 2026 launch.

When can I preload GTA 6?

Digital preload is scheduled to begin on November 12, 2026, one week before launch, so the game is downloaded and ready to play at release.

Is there a disc in the physical box?

Physical copies ship with a download code rather than a game disc. You redeem the code and then download the game.

Is GTA Online available at launch?

Rockstar has described GTA 6 as a single-player experience at launch. A launch date for a separate online mode has not been announced.

Why was GTA 6 delayed?

Rockstar moved the release twice, citing the need for extra development time to finish the game with the level of polish players expect. It shifted from an original 2025 window to May 26, 2026, and then to November 19, 2026.
