About Vice Countdown

Vice Countdown is an independent, fan-run countdown to the release of Grand Theft Auto VI. We exist to answer one deceptively simple question — how long until launch? — accurately, and without the hype and misinformation that surrounds one of the most anticipated games ever made.

Why this site exists

The build-up to a Grand Theft Auto release generates an enormous amount of noise. Fake countdowns, doctored screenshots, made-up "leaked" dates and low-effort content farms all compete for attention. We wanted the opposite: a clean, fast, honest page that shows a live countdown to the officially confirmed date and links every fact back to where it came from.

Our editorial approach

Everything on this site follows a few simple rules:

  • Official sources only. Dates, prices, editions and platform details come from Rockstar Newswire and Take-Two Interactive investor communications — not from social media rumours.
  • We label uncertainty. Anything that has not been officially announced (a PC release, an online launch date) is clearly marked as "not announced" rather than presented as fact.
  • Original words and art. The writing here is our own summary and commentary. The visual design is built entirely from original CSS and SVG — we use no publisher artwork, logos, screenshots or fonts.
  • We correct mistakes. If something changes or we get something wrong, we update the page and the "last updated" date.

Who runs it

Vice Countdown is a small independent project run by a fan of the series, built and maintained on personal time. It is not a company, and it is not connected to any game publisher or platform holder. If you'd like to reach us — a correction, a question, or feedback — the contact page has the details.

How we pay for the site

Running a website costs money. To cover hosting and keep the site free, we display advertising through Google AdSense. Ads are clearly labelled, and they never change what we report. You can read exactly what data advertising involves in our Privacy Policy and Cookie Policy.

A note on trademarks
